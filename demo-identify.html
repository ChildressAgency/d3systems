Understanding the business context, resources that support critical functions, and the related information security risks, we focus our efforts and resources to properly secure the network and cloud resources.

We focus on the following categories:

  • Business context;
  • Resources that support critical functions; and
  • Related information security risks.

ASSET & RESOURCE MANAGEMENT

The Asset Management section of the Information Security Program addresses the data, personnel, devices, systems and facilities that enable an organization to achieve business goals and objectives that are identified and managed consistent with their relative importance to our objectives and rick strategy.

Hardware Management
We maintain accurate inventories of our information systemsn and compnenets.

Software Mangement
We maintain accurate inventories of our approved operating systems and applications.

Data Flow Management
We document our data flows by maintaining network diagrams and Data Flow Diagrams (DFDs).

External Information Systems
We identify and document information systems and services hosted or maintained by third parties.

Resource Value Categorization
We assign assets and resource a classification based on the business value and critaclity in accordance with our policies and standards.

Business Environment

Mission & Objectives
