Designs

Every project starts as a blank page.

Our team collaborates closely with clients to thoroughly understand their research objectives, so that we can advise the most appropriate sampling design and methodology. We assign a team to each project to contextualize the survey instrument and conduct a pre-test, fine-tuning at each point of feedback.

We then coordinate logistics with the local field team to ensure each project is not only appropriate for the target population, but also is implemented strictly adhering to the defined methodology. Where local capacity requires development, we either identify viable partners or invest in local firms to equip them with the skills and tools for completing the project.

Project Orientation

Define Research Objectives

We work closely with our clients to understand their research goals and target audience. We can assist by tailoring research objectives and designing instruments that enable clients to collect actionable data based on their stated goals.

Design Detailed Methodology

We then draft sophisticated Sample Plans that provide detailed explanations of the sample frame, multi-stage probabilities of selection, weighting schemes, and more. We expertly develop scientific methodologies for quantitative, qualitative, and mixed-methods research, in addition to designing experimental and quasi-experimental approaches fro impact evaluations and performance assessments. We can implement surveys, in-depth interviews, focus groups, panels, media monitoring studies, and evaluations, across a variety of modes, including tablets, telephones, pen-and-paper, and online platforms

Determine Accessibility of Target Audience

Our in-house team of statisticians work closely with local field partners to determine the accessibility of the target population and ensure the sample frame accurately reflects any limitations to ensure optimal sampling.

Instrument Design

Contextualize

After receiving the first draft of the questionnaire, we work with the local team to integrate content that is appropriate and relatable to the target audience. When necessary, we conduct cognitive testing to validate questions are perceived in the way they were intended when written.

Translate & Verify

Either through back-translations or third--party verifications, we ensure the local language instrument reflects the original meaning behind the English text, so that nothing is lost in translation.

Pre-Test

Whenever possible, we conduct rigorous pre-tests to implement the survey instrument and assigned methodology. Based on feedback and/or observations, we liaise between clients and the local field partner to make any necessary adjustments.

Data

Reliable, representative data - regardless of the target population

In collaboration with the in-country team, we test the survey instrument and prepare the local staff for implementation, traveling when necessary. We actively communicate with our field partner during data collection and periodically review the results in terms of demographics and survey results. This allows us to provide continuous updates on field progress and limitations to our clients.

Upon receipt of the raw data, we perform rigorous quality control testing and work with the local partner to clarify any items flagged. The delivery of the final data can be in any format desired and will include all weighting variables designed by our statisticians.

When you team with D3, you buy into our commitment to transparency. We provide detailed reports outlining the field procedures, disposition reporting, quality control measures, weighting scheme, and more.

Field Preparation

Convert Instrument into Specified Mode & Test

Our team is well-versed in programming and testing scripts designed for implementation across modes, including via telephone, mobile devices / tablets, and the web. We supplement the scripts by developing detailed interviewer training manuals that highlight how to properly implement both the sampling methodology and survey instrument. For qualitative projects, we either create or help refine discussion and moderator guides so that they effectively inform the research objectives.

Train Field Team

Whenever possible, our team travels in-country to oversee the training of the interviewers, specifically to ensure the sampling techniques and interviewing procedures are correctly communicated to and implemented by the field team.

Field Preparation

Conduct In-field Quality Control

Prior to launching fieldwork, we confirm that the local firm has an adequate plan for monitoring the field procedures, including contact procedures, survey timing, back-checking, and more.

Receive Regular Updates on Achieved Sample Plan

We remain in close contact wiht our local counterparts to receive regular updates on fieldwork, including contact outcomes, demographics of achieved sample, events affecting fieldwork, and more. we are fully transparent and immediately inform clients of notable updates.

Data Processing

Code & Clean Data
