Our Staff

We are proud of our staff's diverse backgrounds and skillsets, enhancing our team's versatility.

The D3 team works across cultural boundaries, speaks multiple languages, and combines field experience with methodological expertise. D3's research staff has decades of experience in research design, data collection, and analysis to inform our clients' strategic decision-making. From complex weighting schemes to regression analysis, our in-house statisticians provide the technical knowledge for designing methodologies that allow clients to achieve their analytical goals. Together, we build an understanding of each client's needs to become true research partners.
